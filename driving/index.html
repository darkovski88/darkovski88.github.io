Product approach

Three traits this studio portfolio consistently signals.

01

Local-language relevance

The strongest pattern across the catalog is proximity: familiar language, familiar formats, and topics that make sense in a Macedonian everyday context.

02

Simple repeat behavior

These apps are not trying to be everything. Each one is organized around a habit: search, browse, guess, answer, or discover.

03

Compact but distinct identities

A dream dictionary and a trivia app should not feel visually identical. The site uses color, rhythm, and card treatment to express those differences.
